Dual Filter Housing Bag

PRODUCT OVERVIEW

Mechmann's Dual Filter Housing Bag systems provide an efficient and reliable solution for liquid filtration across a wide range of industrial applications. Our housings are built to withstand high pressures and temperatures while ensuring minimal maintenance downtime.

MATERIAL SS304 / SS316
MICRON 1 - 200 µm
MAX TEMP Up to 400°C
✔ Industrial Durability
✔ Easy Maintenance
✔ High Flow Rate
✔ Bespoke Designs
Filter Media Polypropylene, Cotton, Glass, Fibre
Housing Core SS304/316, Tin-Plated, PP
Dimensions 10" to 40" length options
Operating Pressure Up to 30 PSID (Differential)
Inlet/Outlet Flanged or Threaded options
